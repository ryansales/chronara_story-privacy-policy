This Privacy Policy explains how Chronara ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information when you use the Chronara Story mobile application ("App"). Please read it carefully. By creating an account or using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

Ryan Sales is the data controller for personal data processed through the App. If you have questions or requests regarding your personal data, contact us at:

  • Email: ryan@salesfamily.net

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom (UK), or Switzerland and wish to raise a concern, you have the right to contact your local supervisory authority.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the App's core features.

Category Specific Data Why Collected
Account Data Email address, encrypted password Authentication and account management
Audio Recordings Voice recordings in M4A/AAC format Core memoir-recording feature
Transcriptions Text transcribed from your recordings Enables search, editing, and export
Story Metadata Story titles, entry dates, timeline ordering data Organizing and chronologically sorting entries
App Settings Audio quality, export format, UI preferences Personalization; stored locally on device only

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, camera images, device identifiers, advertising IDs, financial information, or browsing history. We do not use analytics SDKs or advertising networks.

3. How We Collect Data

  • Directly from you: When you register, record audio, or edit your stories.
  • Automatically during use: Audio files and transcriptions are uploaded to our secure cloud storage as you record.
  • Device permissions: The App requests microphone access (RECORD_AUDIO) only to capture your voice recordings.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve the App:

  • Authenticate you and secure your account.
  • Store and display your recordings and transcriptions.
  • Transcribe audio via OpenAI Whisper (see Third Parties, §6).
  • Chronologically order and clean up your memoir entries via Anthropic Claude AI (see §6).
  • Generate PDF or DOCX memoir exports upon your request.
  • Respond to support requests or legal obligations.

We do not use your data to serve advertisements or sell it to any third party.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA / UK / Switzerland)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we process your personal data under the following legal bases defined in the GDPR (and equivalent UK/Swiss law):

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Account data, audio recordings, transcriptions, and story metadata are processed to deliver the services you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): App settings to improve user experience; security monitoring to protect user data.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): Where required by applicable law.

Audio recordings and transcriptions may constitute special category data if they reveal health, racial/ethnic origin, or other sensitive information (Art. 9 GDPR). You provide this data voluntarily; your explicit consent is obtained at first use. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your content or account.

6. Third-Party Services

We share minimal data with the following sub-processors, each bound by data processing agreements consistent with GDPR requirements:

Service Provider Purpose Data Shared
Database & Authentication Supabase (hosted on AWS) Stores account data, stories, transcriptions, and audio files All user data listed in §2
Audio Transcription OpenAI (Whisper API) Converts audio recordings to text Audio recordings only
AI Ordering & Cleanup Anthropic (Claude API) Chronological ordering and text cleanup of memoir entries Transcription text only

API requests to OpenAI and Anthropic are routed through our server-side Edge Functions. Your API keys and credentials are never exposed to third-party services. All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption.

These providers may process data outside your country. Where applicable (e.g., transfers from the EEA), we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent mechanisms.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Recordings, transcriptions, and stories: Retained until you delete them in-app or delete your account.
  • App settings: Stored locally on your device; cleared when the App is uninstalled.
  • Backups: May be retained up to 90 days after deletion for disaster recovery, after which they are permanently purged.

8. Data Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Audio files at rest are stored in a private Supabase storage bucket accessible only via time-limited signed URLs (1-hour expiration).
  • Database access is restricted by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies — you can only access your own data.
  • The App enforces HTTPS-only network communication (usesCleartextTraffic="false").
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext.
  • Android system backups are disabled for the App to prevent unencrypted data exposure (allowBackup="false").
